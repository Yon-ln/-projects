WEBSITE

You play as the infamous surgeon, Dr Gear. He takes 'orders' from patients with dysfunctional body parts and repairs them. He has to decide who to operate on and then carry out the procedure. the procedure consists of crafting new parts with gears, oils and screws in the right combination. In the meantime, he has to maintain customer satisfaction and pain level of his patient. At the end of each day, Doctor Gear gets scored based on the mean value of customer satisfaction.

Try not to screw up!

S p e c t e r

 

 

A mobile 2.5D puzzle-like game that explores the world of the dead, the main character.

The soul must make their way towards the light to escape the spooky realm.

 

 

 

 

Throughout the making of this, I have learnt quite a few techniques and ideas that go into the development of mobile games. It was important for me to go through the process of understanding the pros and cons of singleton managers and coroutines, it has helped me create a possible workflow in developing my ideas.
